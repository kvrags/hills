What to expect at our estate

Location - We have kept this place natural as we found it.

Kadumane Hills Estate is located on a hill top with a grand view of valley of paddy fields, coffee estates together with forests. We have taken care to preserve this place as natural as we found them. In this natural setting guest cottages are built with modern facilities to make guests comfortable with minimalistic adoptions.

Each of our cottage is equipped with a sit-out overlooking natural valleys. Approximate area of cottages is 15ft x 20ft.

Roads - Our estate is 15km from Saklespura town and well laid concrete roads makes a comfortable drive. The last 1km has estate kaccha roads where the drive takes you in the midst of forests and coffee estates. Before reaching the estate, the drive takes you to cross a shallow stream. Only 4x4 jeep vehicles allowed at this crossing. All other guest vehicles need to be parked before this crossing at the designated parking lot and our estate jeep will take you inside.

Our estate is 300 meters from this parking lot. One can choose to walk this stretch and sure, we'll carry your luggage to cottages.

Campfires

Here at Kadumane Hills we are all about campfires. We think that camping without a campfire is totally crazy and that is why we encourage a safe fire in the evenings. Not only do campfires provide warmth and a focal point for any group or family, but loads of fun too!

We provide a fire pit to every group. We also provide benches and wooden logs around the fire pit so that in the evenings you can sit comfortably and enjoy the warmth. At the same time respect the dangers associated with having campfires.

Here is a quick guide:
1. Only use the fire pits provided.
2. Fire pits should be at least 10 meters away from any tent.
3. Do not collect any wood yourselves!
4. Ashes and embers might still be hot in the morning.
5. Please do not burn anyother materials like paper and plastics in the fire pits.
6. Camp fires are provided only during non-Rainy and non-windy days/seasons.

Please note that the estate management cannot accept responsibility for your possessions or any personal injuries sustained while camping or using fires.
